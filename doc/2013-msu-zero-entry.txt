2013 MSU "Zero-Entry" workshop: Computational Science for Biologists
====================================================================

Titus Brown and Tracy Teal ran this workshop on Sep 23 and Sep 25, 2013,
at Michigan State University.  The workshop was focused on an introduction
to computational science, the shell, the cloud, and IPython Notebook.

The materials are here::

   http://2013-msu-zero-entry.readthedocs.org
